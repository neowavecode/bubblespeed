Welcome to Bubble Speed. This Privacy Policy explains how Jose Jimenez ("we", "us") collects and protects user information. By using Bubble Speed, you agree to these practices.

  • Device Data: Device model, IP address, and Advertising IDs.

3. Purpose of Data Use

  • Authentication: To manage access and save cloud progress.

5. User Rights

Ad Consent: You may manage your ad preferences in the settings.

Data Export & Deletion: You can download your data (JSON) or permanently delete your account and scores via the settings menu.

Our services are not directed to persons under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.
